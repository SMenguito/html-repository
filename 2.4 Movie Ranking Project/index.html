The Best Movies According to Silvester

My top 3 movies of all time.

Top Gun: Maverick

I love this movie because the movie highlights the bravery of our aviators and sailors at sea. It is indeed a love letter to aviation.

Although this film does offer plenty of style, it's pretty clear that the creators chose substance over style, focusing on strong
characters, compelling writing, and excellent direction, rather than formulaic presentations or mimicry.

Tears of the Sun

This is a very heroic film dedicated to soldiers. Giving importance to the lives of fellowmen who are in need especially
those who are in the hands of bad people.

“It's a movie about humanity, finding that human side in all of us in the middle of conflict,” star Bruce Willis (Waters) says. “Waters
embarks not only on a physical journey, but also on a soulful journey. You see him transformed by the compassion he feels for these
refugees and their plight.

Olympus Has Fallen

This is a very action-packed movie of Gerard Butler It happens to be that he is my favorite action star.

While "Olympus Has Fallen" breaks no major new ground in the political thriller genre, Fuqua has directed a sharp, very taut adventure
that keeps you engrossed from start to finish. It delivers some excellent visuals and a few surprising twists that add beautifully to the suspense.
